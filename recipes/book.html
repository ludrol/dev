How to make a book

Description

Book-gremlins are mischievous creatures that have a love for books and a talent for creating them. They are often found lurking in libraries and bookstores, sneaking about and adding their own unique touches to the books they come across. Some book-gremlins are known for adding witty comments in the margins, while others enjoy creating hidden illustrations throughout the pages. They are often credited with bringing life and personality to otherwise dull books, and are highly sought after by book lovers for their unique and creative touches. Whether you come across a book-gremlin's work by chance or actively seek out their creations, you can be sure that it will be a one-of-a-kind reading experience.

  • letters
